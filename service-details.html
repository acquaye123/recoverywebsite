Occupational Health

Comprehensive Health Surveillance Services

Ensuring safe, compliant, and productive workplaces

At Recovery Workplace Health, we provide tailored health surveillance programs to help businesses identify, monitor, and manage occupational health risks. From routine screenings to specialist assessments, we support your duty of care obligations while improving employee wellbeing.

Our team works with employers across sectors to meet regulatory requirements, reduce sickness absence, and foster a healthier workforce. By taking a proactive approach, we protect both employees and the long-term sustainability of your business.

Our Services Include:

  • Lung function testing (spirometry)
  • Hearing assessments (audiometry)
  • Vision and eye screening
  • Skin checks for occupational dermatitis
  • Vaccination & immunisation programs
  • Fitness-for-work medicals
