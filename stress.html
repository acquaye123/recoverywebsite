Mental Health & Wellbeing

Comprehensive Stress & Wellbeing Support

Creating healthier, more resilient workplaces

At Recovery Workplace Health, we understand the impact stress and poor mental wellbeing can have on both employees and organisations. Our Stress & Wellbeing Support services are designed to help employees build resilience, manage pressures, and thrive in the workplace.

By offering proactive support and targeted interventions, we help reduce sickness absence, improve employee satisfaction, and create a healthier organisational culture where people feel supported and valued.

Our Services Include:

  • Stress risk assessments
  • Resilience and wellbeing workshops
  • One-to-one employee wellbeing consultations
  • Return-to-work mental health support
  • Advice on workplace adjustments
  • Mental health awareness training for managers
