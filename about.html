About Us

We understand the vital link between employee wellbeing and business performance. A workforce that feels supported and cared for is more engaged, efficient, and committed. By partnering with RWPH, organisations not only protect their people but also unlock higher levels of productivity and growth.

Committed to Excellence in Healthcare

At RWPH, we understand the powerful link between employee well-being and organisational performance. When people feel supported, valued, and well cared for, they are more engaged, resilient, and productive. That’s why organisations choose RWPH as a trusted occupational health partner because we provide clear, confident, and evidence-based guidance that genuinely makes a difference.

We combine clinical expertise with practical workplace insight to deliver tailored solutions that enhance wellbeing, reduce risk, and strengthen organisational capability. Our approach is built on clarity, trust, and meaningful support, empowering businesses to protect their people while unlocking higher levels of productivity, stability, and sustainable growth. With RWPH, you don’t just meet your duty of care. You elevate the performance and potential of your entire workforce.

Afua Baffour - Managing Director

As Managing Director, I provide strategic leadership and direction for Recovery Workplace Limited, ensuring the organisation delivers high-quality workplace health and wellbeing solutions that meet the evolving needs of businesses.

With extensive experience in occupational health and workplace wellbeing, I lead the development of evidence-based services, oversee clinical governance, and ensure compliance with relevant health, safety, and regulatory standards across all operations.

I work closely with clients, partners, and internal teams to build strong relationships, drive service excellence, and design solutions that promote safer working environments, employee wellbeing, and sustainable organisational performance.
